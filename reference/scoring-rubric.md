# scoring-rubric.md — The 5-Dimension Score

For leads that clear the gate. Assign five 0–10 dimensions, compute the composite, read the grade, route by grade. Score before you spend — no drafted outreach on an ungraded lead.

---

## The 5 dimensions and weights (sum 100)

| Dimension | Weight | The signal it reads |
|---|---|---|
| **Need** | **30%** | How badly the business needs what we sell — missing/weak web presence, no booking, visible gaps. |
| **ICP Fit** | **25%** | Match to our ideal client — local service business, right size, right vertical (not chain, not solo hobby). |
| **Reach** | **20%** | Can we actually reach the decision-maker — phone present, named owner, responsive. |
| **Pay** | **15%** | Signals they can afford $750+ — established, busy, revenue proxies (reviews, employees). |
| **Intent** | **10%** | Signs they want to grow now — hiring, expanding, recent investment, seasonal surge. |

**Need outranks fit; fit outranks everything else.** For a cold, local, low-ticket pipeline, a business that visibly *needs* the work closes faster than a content perfect-ICP. Weighting is deliberate.

---

## 0–10 anchors per dimension

Score against the concrete observation, not a vibe. Cite one piece of evidence per score.

### Need (30%) — *no/weak website scores HIGH need*
| Score | Anchor |
|---|---|
| 0 | Polished recent site, full booking, everything works. No gap to sell into. |
| 3 | Decent site, minor gaps (no clear CTA, dated copy). Low web need — pivot to the AI-receptionist angle for the real pain. |
| 6 | Real gaps: phone-only booking, broken form, no mobile, or high call-volume business (HVAC, 200+ reviews) missing calls. |
| 9 | No site at all, or an obviously broken/missing-info site. Acute, namable pain. |

### ICP Fit (25%)
| Score | Anchor |
|---|---|
| 0 | Chain, franchise, retail/food with no service need, or out-of-lane vertical. |
| 3 | Service-ish but wrong size (solo hobby, or too sophisticated/premium-agency territory). |
| 6 | Local service business, right size, acceptable vertical. |
| 9 | Sweet-spot: local trade/service (contractor, salon, dental, etc.), owner-operated, the studio's home turf. |

### Reach (20%)
| Score | Anchor |
|---|---|
| 0 | No phone, gatekeeper-walled, no path to the signer. |
| 3 | Phone exists but no owner name; main line only. |
| 6 | Phone works, owner discoverable, plausible to reach. |
| 9 | Owner named, direct line or responsive, answers their own phone. |

### Pay (15%)
| Score | Anchor |
|---|---|
| 0 | No revenue proxies; signals below the $750 floor. |
| 3 | Thin proxies — few reviews, solo, low-activity. |
| 6 | Established: steady reviews, multiple employees, going concern. |
| 9 | Clearly busy and capitalized — high review count, multi-employee, premium market. |

### Intent (10%)
| Score | Anchor |
|---|---|
| 0 | Static, no growth signal, possibly winding down. |
| 3 | Operating but flat — no visible growth motion. |
| 6 | Some motion — recent reviews, seasonal surge, minor expansion. |
| 9 | Actively investing — hiring, new location, recent funding, expanding now. |

## Composite and grades

```
composite = Σ ( dim/10 × weight )      → rounded to an integer 0–100
```

| Grade | Composite |
|---|---|
| **A+** | ≥ 90 |
| **A** | 80–89 |
| **B** | 65–79 |
| **C** | 50–64 |
| **D** | < 50 |

---

## Grade → routing

| Grade | Route | Action |
|---|---|---|
| **A+ / A** | **Call first** | Best ROI. Draft the call angle now, prioritize. |
| **B** | **Calling rotation** | Draft outreach, queue it. |
| **C / D** | **Nurture** | Draft the low-cost nurture touch. Don't spend a call. **Never warm-pitch a D.** |

A **lean-in signal** (`walk-away-flags.md`) overrides the grade upward — say why the signal beats the score. The rubric is the default, not the ceiling.

## Intake-form 5-signal bands (when a lead came through the form, not scraped)

Capture as **ranges, never open fields** — people answer honestly when given ranges. This is the warm-lead version of the score; cross-check against the walk-away criteria regardless of total.

| Signal | Bands | Points |
|---|---|---|
| **Budget** | under $1.5k / $1.5–3.5k / $3.5–7k / $7k+ | >$7k = 25 · $3.5–7k = 20 · $1.5–3.5k = 12 · under = 0 |
| **Timeline** | immediately / within 30 days / 1–3 months / just exploring | immediately = 20 · ≤30d = 15 · 1–3mo = 8 · exploring = 0 |
| **Decision authority** | Yes / Shared / No | Yes = 20 · Shared = 10 · No = 0 |
| **Core problem** | 1–2 sentences (clarity = signal) | clear/specific = 15 |
| **Prior attempts** | Yes/No + brief (engaged buyer) | tried something = 10 |
| **Service fit** | matches a studio line | fits = 10 |

**Intake routing (max 100):**
- **65+** → High → personal reply + diagnostic booking link, **within minutes**.
- **35–64** → Medium → 3-touch nurture over 7–10 days.
- **<35** → Low → one warm decline + a free resource, single send.

**Warm/referred leads skip scoring** — the relationship establishes qualification; route straight to the diagnostic. Scoring informs; the diagnostic still diagnoses. Never prescribe from the score alone.
